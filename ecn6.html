About

Ramil Laguda is a passionate and dedicated person from Silang and the owner of ECN Barbershop from Perez, Trece Martires City, whose love for frying has been a lifelong journey. Growing up, Ramil was inspired by the art of hairdressing and was fascinated by how a simple hairstyle can change a person's appearance and boost their confidence. This passion never waned even as he took on various jobs to support his growing family.

Ramil is a devoted family man, blessed with a beautiful and hardworking wife Nancy and five wonderful children: Nadine, Ryza, Cj, April and Ethan. His family was his greatest source of inspiration and motivation. Despite the challenges of supporting a large family, Ramil always held onto his dream of opening his own hair salon.

With the unwavering support of Nancy and their children, Ramil finally decided to realize his dream. His hair salon is more than just a business; it is the culmination of years of dedication, hard work and the pursuit of a lifelong passion. Ramil's barber shop is a testament to his love for craftsmanship and his commitment to creating a better future for his family. This is where skill meets art and where every customer leaves their best self.
